Luke 21 - A Prophetic Break

Sun, 22 Mar 2026 17:59:04 GMT • From feed: https://rss.com/podcasts/gloucestervineyard/2649576

Overall theme

The podcast discusses the theme of seasons, both in nature and in the life of the church. It reflects on the importance of recognising the current season and preparing for the next one, particularly as the church is about to launch a new site. The conversation encourages listeners to engage with the changes ahead, fostering hope and excitement for the future. It also highlights the need for community involvement and individual action in this transitional phase.

Key quotations

  • “We are moving into a season of harvest, a season to be activated, a season to push forwards and to be bold.”

Bible passages

  • Luke 21:29-31
  • Matthew 5:13-16

Questions you may wish to reflect on

  • What does it mean to recognise the season we are in as a church?
  • How can we prepare for the new season ahead?
  • What actions can we take to be more engaged in our community?
  • In what ways can we support each other during this transition?
  • How can we encourage more people to explore faith within our church?

Further reading

  • Ecclesiastes 3:1-8 — This passage speaks about the different seasons in life and the importance of timing, which aligns with the discussion on recognising and acting according to the seasons we experience.
  • Isaiah 43:18-19 — This passage encourages us to look forward to new things that God is doing, which resonates with the theme of moving into a new season as a church.
